Frequently asked questions

How do I book a session or class?

You can easily book all gym sessions and classes directly through our website's online booking portal after registering for an account.

What types of classes do you offer?

We offer a diverse range of classes including HIIT, yoga, spin, strength training, and functional fitness, suitable for all fitness levels and goals.

Are personal trainers available at Rumion Gym?

Yes, certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one sessions and personalized program design. You can book a consultation through our online system.

What are your membership options and prices?

Rumion Gym offers flexible monthly, quarterly, and annual membership plans, each designed to provide great value for our premium features and amenities.

Can I try the gym before committing to a membership?

Absolutely! We offer a complimentary trial pass for new members to experience our facilities and a class before joining Rumion Gym.
